How We Work

Lab First

Every engagement starts in the lab. We research the problem, build a solution, stress-test it, and document everything. No guesswork. No vendor hand-waving. Just methodical engineering grounded in real protocol behavior.

01

Research

Dig into the protocols, read the RFCs, analyze the packet captures. Understand the problem at the deepest level before touching a single config.

02

Build

Stand up the architecture in the lab. Real hardware, real software, real configurations. Replicate the production environment as closely as possible.

03

Break

Intentionally stress, fault-inject, and edge-case test every component. If it breaks in the lab, it would have broken in production. Find it first.

04

Document

Produce clear, thorough documentation that any engineer can follow. Configs, diagrams, decision rationale. The work speaks for itself.
