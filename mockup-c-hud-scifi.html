Approach

Lab First

Every engagement starts in the lab. We research it, build it, break it, then document it — before it ever touches production.

01
Research

Deep-dive into protocols, RFCs, and vendor implementations. Understand the problem space before writing a single config.

02
Build

Stand up real infrastructure in the lab. Physical gear, virtual environments, full-stack configurations.

03
Break

Attack it. Stress-test it. Find the edge cases and failure modes before an adversary does.

04
Document

Publish findings, configs, and methodologies. Knowledge that doesn't get documented doesn't scale.
