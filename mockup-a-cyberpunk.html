methodology

Lab First

Every engagement starts in the lab. We don’t guess at solutions — we build, test, and validate before anything touches production.

01

Research

Investigate the problem space. Read the RFCs, study the protocols, map the threat model. Understand before acting.

02

Build

Stand it up in the lab. Prototype configurations, deploy test infrastructure, create a working proof of concept.

03

Break

Stress test everything. Find the failure modes, edge cases, and bypass opportunities before an attacker does.

04

Document

Record every finding. Deliver clear, actionable documentation that your team can execute on independently.
